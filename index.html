Hello! My name is Raúl Moroni Capcha Cadillo. I am from the city of Huánuco, Perú. I like to dance, and I usually do it in my free time. I also enjoy listening to music and watching movies. Another important part of my life is that I served a full-time mission in Peru-Limatambo Mission.

About Peru

Peru is a country in South America known for its rich culture, history, and breathtaking landscapes. From Machu Picchu to the Amazon rainforest and the Andes mountains, Peru is a land full of wonders. Huánuco, my city, is located in the central highlands and is famous for its warm climate and traditions.
